# Signal Resume Hybrid

## Concept name
Signal Resume Hybrid

## Visual mood
Sharp, efficient, and credibility-first. This direction blends the cleanliness of a modern personal site with the fast signal of a strong resume page.

## Layout structure
- Split hero: short bio on one side, key metrics or highlights on the other
- Immediate access to resume, LinkedIn, and GitHub
- Timeline or stacked sections for experience and project highlights
- Compact testimonials, tools, or focus areas if desired
- Clear CTA to get in touch

## Key sections
- Hero with headline and quick links
- Highlights / metrics / focus areas
- Experience snapshot
- Project highlights
- Resume download and LinkedIn
- Contact

## Color and typography direction
- Palette: crisp white, dark graphite, cool blue-gray, minimal accent in teal or indigo
- Typography: highly legible sans-serif system with strong weight contrast
- UI cues: clean dividers, compact badges, minimal animation

## Strengths and tradeoffs
### Strengths
- Very effective for hiring managers with limited time
- Communicates professionalism and readiness quickly
- Easier to keep current over time

### Tradeoffs
- Less expressive than the more artful or editorial directions
- Can feel slightly corporate if the brand voice is too restrained

## Best fit
Best if Skyler wants the portfolio to double as a highly usable professional landing page.
